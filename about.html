About
A responsive web application that analyzes and displays instant ramen statistics to help college students find the best ramen for them
Our Inspiration
All over the world, college students pay tens of thousands for tuition, dorming, and daily expenses. One staple item that keeps our stomaches full, energy boosted, and wallets safe is instant ramen.When we saw that our final INF 133 project allowed us to create anything, we instantly thought about creating a website for college students to see the variety of instant ramen in the world.
Using a large ramen review dataset from The Ramen Rater, we plan to give meaning to the data by including what each graph means and what are the recommendations of that information. This will help college students pick what their next meal and provide insightful knowledge to the different cultures, brands, and flavors!
Our Dedication to Accessbility
In efforts to make our website accessible, inclusive, and helpful to all users, we have incorporated:
  • The A11y Project Content Style Guide
  • The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)
Additionally, our site passes:
  • W3C Markup Validation Service
  • AChecker Web Accessibility Checker
